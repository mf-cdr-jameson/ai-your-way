# Where to use your AI Working Style — tool by tool

Once you've got your **"My AI Working Style"** block (see `portable-profile-template.md`), here's where to paste it in each tool so the AI follows it automatically. General-purpose; no special access needed.

---

## Claude Code (the CLI / IDE)
- You don't need to paste anything — this skill applies the style live, and can **save it to memory** so future sessions start that way automatically.
- Helpful built-ins: **plan mode** (think before doing on bigger tasks), and asking Claude to keep a **checklist / done-list** for long work.

## Claude — Cowork
- Paste your block at the **start of a session**, or keep it in a doc you reference, so the assistant follows your style for that piece of work.

## Claude — Chat & Projects
- For a one-off chat: paste the block as your **first message**.
- For ongoing work: create a **Project** and put the block in the Project's **custom instructions / knowledge** so every chat in it inherits your style.

## Google Gemini
- Create a **Gem**: give it a name like "Works the way I do" and paste your block as the Gem's instructions. Start chats from that Gem and it'll follow your style.
- Or paste the block at the top of a normal chat for a one-off.

## ChatGPT
- Put your block in **Settings → Personalization → Custom instructions** (the "How would you like ChatGPT to respond?" box) so it applies everywhere.
- Or paste it as the first message for a single chat.

---

**Tip:** keep your block in a note you can grab quickly. Tweak it whenever something isn't working — it's yours, and it's meant to change as you learn what helps.
